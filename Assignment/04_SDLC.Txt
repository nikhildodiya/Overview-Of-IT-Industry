SDLC Means Software Developement Life Cycle.. It's Work In Six Steps..

1. Requirement Gethering.. 

The need of the customer has to be understood. What type of requirement is there in customer software? What kind of software do you make?

2. Analysis

Analysis Means One Kind Of The Market Survey..According to the needs of the customer, what is going on in the market and how it works.. such a survey is done in the market. It is called Analysis

3. Design

Designing is made according to the requirement of the customer.

4.implementation

implementation means to start coding..

5. Testing

After the software is built, testing is done by the testing team. There is no bug or error of any kind and it is tested

6.maintenance

Updating the software is called maintenance
